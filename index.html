Title of the Section

Cherry Blossoms in Korea

Sub-Title

Spring is a very special time in South Korea because it’s when the cherry blossom trees burst into bloom. It’s a spectacular sight that you won’t want to miss.

Other sub-title

Cherry blossoms in Korea come out from mid March until early April.

Other sub-title

The first blossoms of the cherry blossom season in Korea always start in the south on Jeju Island and slowly work their way up towards Seoul, entering full bloom by early April.

Other sub-title

Most popular events in South Korea is the magnificent cherry blossom festival. Marking the transformation from winter to spring, there are a number of destinations across the peninsula where visitors can wonder at the beauty of the beotkkot, or cherry blossom, and soak up some spring sunshine at the same time.
